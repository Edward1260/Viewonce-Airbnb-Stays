Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel a booking?

Go to "My Bookings" → Find your booking → Click "Cancel Booking". Review the cancellation policy before confirming.

What payment methods are accepted?

Credit/Debit Cards, M-Pesa, PayPal, Bank Transfer, and Cash on Arrival. All payments are secure.

How do I contact my host?

Use the Messages section in your dashboard. You can chat or call hosts directly.

How do refunds work?

Refunds follow the property's cancellation policy. Full refunds for cancellations 24+ hours before check-in.
